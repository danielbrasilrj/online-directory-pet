Revenue Route

How the Money Flows

End-to-end from zero to passive income. No cold outreach, no contacting anyone.

1

Scrape Google Maps

Automated script pulls 20K-40K pet service listings (name, address, phone, rating, hours) from Google Maps. Zero human contact. Cost: ~$50-100 via Outscraper.

2

Clean & Enrich with Claude Code

Feed CSVs to Claude Code. Remove junk (closed, duplicates, off-niche). Enrich top listings by crawling their websites for services, specialties, photos. 50K raw → 20K clean.

3

Generate 2,000+ SEO Pages

Programmatic pages: "Best Dog Groomers in Austin TX", "Pet Sitters in Chicago". Each page targets a "[service] in [city]" keyword. State pages rank for 1,000+ terms each.

4

Google Indexes → Traffic Arrives

SEO compounds over 2-4 months. Organic traffic grows from 0 to 5K-15K visits/mo by month 6. No ads needed, no marketing budget.

5

Revenue Streams Kick In

AdSense: $150-400/mo on traffic pages. Featured Listings: providers find their free listing, pay $99-199/yr to upgrade. Affiliate: pet insurance, Chewy, Rover = $150-300/mo. You never contact anyone — they come to you.

Niche Validation Tools

Before building, confirm demand exists. These are the tools used in the Greg Isenberg video to validate directory niches (funeral homes, senior living, GasBuddy). Use them to score our pet niche.

Primary

Ahrefs

Keyword research + competitor traffic analysis. Find "near me" keywords with 30-100K searches and KD < 20. Spy on competitor directories.

Traffic

SimilarWeb

Estimate competitor directory traffic. Used in the video to guess funeral home directory visits. Compare pet directory competitors.

Free

Official

Official Google search volumes. Free with Google Ads account. The most accurate volume data since it's from Google itself.

Decisions

What You Need to Decide

Before starting Phase 1, settle these questions.

Which Market First?

USA has higher search volumes and AdSense RPM. Brazil has less competition and you know the market. You could do both with i18n, but pick one to focus data seeding first.

Must decide before Phase 2

All Pet Services or One?

Start with all 8 categories (groomers, vets, sitters, etc.) for more pages and broader SEO, or go deep on one first (e.g., only dog groomers) for faster quality.

Recommendation: all 8

Data Source Budget?

Outscraper ($50-100) gives you 50K+ listings fast. Google Places API is cheaper but slower. Or scrape manually for free but it takes 10x longer.

Recommendation: Outscraper $50-100

Domain Name?

Pick a domain that targets your niche keyword. Examples: PetServicesNearMe.com, FindMyGroomer.com, PetCareDirectory.com. Check availability and buy before starting.

Must decide before Phase 1
